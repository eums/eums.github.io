<h1>Contact Us<&#47;h1><br />
<img class="size-full wp-image-620" src="http:&#47;&#47;eums.eusa.ed.ac.uk&#47;wp-content&#47;uploads&#47;build&#47;contact.jpg" alt="" width="620" height="182" &#47;></p>
<p>We do our best as a society to communicate with our members and the wider community, face to face, through email and by posting updates on our website or on social media. We also love to hear back from you, whether it be compliment, comment or complaint.<&#47;p></p>
<p>There are a number of ways that you can get in contact with the society:<&#47;p></p>
<h2><strong>IN PERSON<&#47;strong><&#47;h2></p>
<p>You can speak to any one of our dedicated ensemble managers in person by coming along to their respective rehearsals. Details of the rehearsal times can be found on our ensemble pages for <a title="More details about Chorus" href="http:&#47;&#47;eums.eusa.ed.ac.uk&#47;society&#47;biography&#47;chorus">Chorus<&#47;a>, <a title="More details about Sinfonia" href="http:&#47;&#47;eums.eusa.ed.ac.uk&#47;society&#47;biography&#47;sinfonia">Sinfonia<&#47;a> and the <a title="More details about the Symphony Orchestra" href="http:&#47;&#47;eums.eusa.ed.ac.uk&#47;society&#47;biography&#47;symphony">Symphony Orchestra<&#47;a>, and locations can be found on our <a title="Rehearsal locations" href="http:&#47;&#47;eums.eusa.ed.ac.uk&#47;find&#47;practice">Find Us<&#47;a> page.<&#47;p></p>
<h2><strong>BY EMAIL<&#47;strong><&#47;h2></p>
<p>The quickest way to get a response from us if you cannot come and speak to us in person is by emailing us at <a title="Email us" href="mailto:secretary@eums.org.uk" target="_blank">secretary@eums.org.uk<&#47;a>.<&#47;p></p>
